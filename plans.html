FAQ

Plan & Pricing Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions about our plans and billing.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and we'll prorate the difference so you only pay for what you use. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties, no paperwork.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. Every SecureShield plan includes a 15-day free-look period as mandated by IRDAI. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll refund 100% of the premium paid — no questions asked, no deductions.

Do you offer family discounts?

Yes! Family floater plans offer 15–25% savings compared to buying individual policies for each family member. The more family members covered, the greater the value. Our Standard and Premium plans are specifically designed for families.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and EMI options via select banks. All transactions are 256-bit SSL encrypted.

Is the yearly plan billed as one payment or monthly?

The yearly plan is billed as a single annual payment upfront, which is how you get the 20% discount. If you prefer spreading the cost, you can stay on the monthly plan or contact us about quarterly billing options for Enterprise.

Not Sure Which Plan Is Right for You?

Talk to one of our insurance advisors. We'll understand your needs and recommend the exact plan — completely free, no sales pressure.
