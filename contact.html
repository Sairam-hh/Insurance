Quick Help

Common Questions

Before reaching out, check if your question is already answered below.

How quickly will I hear back after submitting this form?

Our team responds to all enquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent claim matters, please call our 24/7 claims helpline at 1800 999 9999 for an immediate response.

Can I get a call back instead of sending a message?

Absolutely. Mention your preferred callback time in the message field and our advisor will call you within that window. Alternatively, call our toll-free number 1800 123 4567 directly.

I already have a policy — who should I contact for renewal?

Existing policyholders can renew directly through their SecureShield dashboard after logging in. For assistance, email renewals@secureshield.in or call your dedicated relationship manager if you're on the Enterprise plan.

Is the toll-free number available on weekends?

Our claims helpline (1800 999 9999) operates 24/7, including all weekends and public holidays. The general enquiry line (1800 123 4567) is available Monday–Friday 9AM–9PM and Saturday 10AM–6PM.
