Our Story

25 Years of Protecting Indian Families

Founded in 2000, SecureShield began with a simple mission: make insurance accessible, transparent, and affordable for every Indian household. From a small Mumbai office to serving 10 million customers across India, our commitment has never wavered.

Today we partner with India's top insurers to offer 200+ plans across 6 categories, all backed by an industry-leading 99% claim settlement ratio.
