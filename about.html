Our Story

25 Years of Protecting Indian Families

Founded in Mumbai in the year 2000, SecureShield began with a simple yet powerful mission — to make insurance truly accessible, transparent and affordable for every Indian household, regardless of income or location.

From a 5-person team working out of a small office in Andheri to serving over 10 million customers across every state in India, our commitment to that original mission has never wavered. We've grown, but we haven't changed.

Today, we partner with India's top 15 licensed insurers to offer 200+ plans across 6 categories — all backed by an industry-leading 99% claim settlement ratio and a promise to be there when you need us most.

Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Two guiding principles that shape every decision we make — from the plans we design to the way we handle every claim.

Our Mission

To empower every Indian with affordable, transparent insurance that genuinely protects what matters most — without confusing paperwork, without hidden clauses, and without compromise on service quality. We believe protection is a right, not a privilege.

Our Vision

To become the most loved and trusted insurance brand in India by 2030 — known not just for our products, but for the genuine peace of mind we bring to every household we serve, powered by technology and rooted in empathy.
