FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you get covered.

How quickly can I get a policy after purchase?

Most motor and travel policies are issued instantly the moment payment is confirmed. Health and life insurance policies are typically issued within 1–2 business days, as they may require a brief medical questionnaire or verification step.

What is your claim settlement ratio?

SecureShield maintains a 99% claim settlement ratio across all insurance categories — one of the highest in the Indian insurance industry. This figure is audited annually and published in our annual report.

Can I customise my plan with add-ons?

Absolutely. Every plan supports a wide range of add-ons and riders — including critical illness cover, zero depreciation for motor, maternity benefits, and international coverage for travel. Our advisors can help you build exactly what you need.

Do you offer cashless treatment at hospitals?

Yes. We have a network of 500+ empanelled hospitals across India offering complete cashless treatment. Simply present your SecureShield health card at the network hospital — no upfront payment required.

Is my existing condition covered under health plans?

Pre-existing conditions are typically covered after a standard waiting period of 2–4 years, as per IRDAI guidelines. Our advisors will walk you through exactly what applies to your situation during your free consultation.
